biography

Mark Jordan Manner lives and writes in Toronto.

His stories have appeared in Grain, EVENT, Prairie Fire, The Fiddlehead, PRISM international, Existere, The Antigonish Review, Riddle Fence, The Puritan, The Feathertale Review, The Dalhousie Review, Paper Darts, Little Fiction, Ricepaper, Echolocation, The Impressment Gang, Word Riot, , untethered and others.

His comic zine, entitled You Are Someone Who Exists, was launched in Summer ’13.

He has a BA in Creative Writing and English from York University where he received the 2011 President’s Prize for Fiction, 2012 Prize for Stage Play, and 2013 Prizes for both Fiction and Stage Play, as well as the Babs Burggraf Award in Creative Writing.

He is currently completing his MFA at The University of Guelph.

Mark recently completed his first novel.
